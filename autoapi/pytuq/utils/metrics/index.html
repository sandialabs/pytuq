pytuq.utils.metrics.crps_samples(y, samples)[source]

Compute CRPS from predictive samples.

Parameters:

  • y (np.ndarray) – true array of shape (M,) or scalar

  • samples (np.ndarray) – predictive samples of shape (M, N) or (N,).

Returns:

CRPS metric of shape (M,)

Return type:

np.ndarray

Notes

Empirical CRPS: CRPS = mean(|X - y|) - 0.5 * mean(|X_i - X_j|) where X_i are Monte Carlo samples from the predictive distribution.
